Types of Various Wedding Invitation Card Services

Boxed Invitation Cards - The tradition of boxed indian wedding invitation card is old but getting popular again in modern times. Distributing boxed invites is the latest trend and including sweets, chocolates, or other wedding goodies inside the box just makes it appear more lavish.

Unboxed Invitation Cards - These are the traditional wedding invitation cards which can be custom-made according to your preferences. They are artistic masterpieces in their own way and are comparatively less expensive than the boxed ones. Designs are traditional, modern, innovative and interesting.

Digital E-invite - In this digital age, most couples are also opting for digital e-invitation cards. They are a fun and practical way to invite relatives, friends and colleagues as you can send them over Whatsapp or Facebook in a single click.
